Getting to know a little about Brazil

  1. The soccer: Five times world cup champion.
  2. The music: Samba and Bossa Nova
  3. The tourism: Beaches, Amazon Fores, Rio de Janeiro and Bahia

Brazil is the largest county in South America and the fifth largest in the world and it has the biggest Portuguese-speaking population.

Brazil has 26 states and one Federal District which holds the capital city.
